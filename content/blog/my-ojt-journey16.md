# March 24,2026

* Title: Arrival in Cebu and First Company Visit

Today, we arrived in Cebu City for our IT industry visit. I felt excited because this was a chance to see how real companies operate outside of our usual classroom environment.

Our first visit was to MATA Inc., a company focused on Virtual Reality (VR). They introduced us to how VR is used in real-world applications, not just for games but also for training and simulations.

Seeing their work made me realize how immersive technology is becoming part of modern systems. It inspired me to think about how my own projects could evolve beyond traditional web applications.

# March 25, 2026

* Title: Understanding the BPO Industry

Today, we visited Teleperformance, a well-known company in the BPO (Business Process Outsourcing) industry.

They explained how their systems handle customer service operations on a large scale. I was surprised by how organized and structured their workflows are, especially in handling multiple clients and users at once.

This visit helped me understand that IT is not only about development but also about communication, efficiency, and managing real-time services for people around the world.


# March 26, 2026

* Today was very technical and interesting because we visited two companies. First was Rivan IT, where we learned about network engineering. They showed how networks are designed, maintained, and secured in real environments.

After that, we visited Living Life, a company focused on Internet of Things (IoT). They demonstrated how devices can connect and communicate to automate tasks.

These visits made me realize how important infrastructure and connectivity are. Without proper networking and IoT systems, modern applications would not function effectively.

# March 27, 2026 

* Title: Reflection on Industry Experience

Today, I reflected on everything we experienced during our stay in Cebu City. Each company showed a different side of the IT industry—from VR, BPO, networking, to IoT.

This experience opened my mind to the many career paths in IT. It also made me realize that the skills I am currently developing, like programming and system design, can be applied in many different fields.

Overall, this trip was not just educational but also motivating. It gave me a clearer vision of where I want to go in the future as an IT professional.
